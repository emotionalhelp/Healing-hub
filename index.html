🧠 Understanding Mental Health

Mental health affects how we think, feel, and act. It’s just as important as physical health. Feeling overwhelmed or low sometimes is part of being human — but you don’t have to go through it alone.

⚠️ Signs You Might Need Support

• Constant overthinking
• Feeling sad or empty
• Trouble sleeping
• Loss of interest in things you love
• Feeling anxious or restless

🌱 Simple Ways to Feel Better

• Take slow deep breaths
• Talk to someone you trust
• Write down your thoughts
• Take small breaks
• Be kind to yourself
